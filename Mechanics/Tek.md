## <a name="tek"></a>Tek

To Tek: Hold J or press J at the right moment after being knocked down.
Tek will make it so you can't be knocked down or grabbed by beefies.
The idle animation will get rid of tek.

Common usages:
- Arena
- Lava world boss
- Necro
